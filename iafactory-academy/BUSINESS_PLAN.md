## 9. RISK ANALYSIS & MITIGATION

### Key Risks

**Risk 1: Competition from large players**
- **Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** High
- **Mitigation:** 
  - Focus on francophone niche (defensible moat)
  - Build community (harder to replicate)
  - Move fast to establish brand

**Risk 2: Content becomes outdated quickly**
- **Probability:** High | **Impact:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**
  - Update curriculum quarterly
  - Focus on timeless principles
  - Create "news" content series

**Risk 3: Low student engagement/completion**
- **Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**
  - Build community features early
  - Offer certificates/credentials
  - Gamification (streaks, levels)
  - 1:1 support for engaged students

**Risk 4: Difficult to acquire customers profitably**
- **Probability:** Low | **Impact:** High
- **Mitigation:**
  - Free course as lead magnet
  - Organic content marketing (SEO, YouTube)
  - Strategic partnerships
  - Referral program with incentives

**Risk 5: Inability to build at scale**
- **Probability:** Low | **Impact:** High
- **Mitigation:**
  - Hire ops team early
  - Automate workflows
  - Use no-code platforms where possible
  - Consider white-label platform (LearnDash, Teachable)

**Risk 6: Payment processing issues (France/Africa)**
- **Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**
  - Multiple payment gateways (Stripe, Wise, local options)
  - Support multiple currencies
  - Local partnerships for regional payment

**Risk 7: Regulatory/compliance issues**
- **Probability:** Low | **Impact:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**
  - GDPR compliance (Switzerland-friendly)
  - Consumer protection laws (research per country)
  - Refund policy clear and generous
  - Legal review before scale
