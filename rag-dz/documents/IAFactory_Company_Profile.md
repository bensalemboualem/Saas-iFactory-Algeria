\# IAFactory - Company Profile



\## Who We Are



IAFactory is a cutting-edge AI and SaaS solutions company with dual presence in \*\*Algeria\*\* and \*\*Switzerland (Geneva)\*\*, delivering enterprise-grade technology solutions to clients across Africa, Europe, and the Middle East.



\*\*Founded\*\*: 2023

\*\*Locations\*\*: 

\- IAFactory Algeria (Headquarters)

\- IAFactory Switzerland (Geneva)



\*\*Mission\*\*: Democratize AI and modern SaaS technologies for businesses and institutions in emerging markets while maintaining Swiss-grade quality standards.
