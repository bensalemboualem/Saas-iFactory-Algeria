### Do you work with international clients?
Yes, we serve clients in Switzerland and internationally with multilingual support in English, French, German, Italian, and Arabic.

### Can you build a chatbot in my language?
Yes, we develop multilingual chatbots in English, French, German, Italian, Arabic, and other major languages.

### What's the difference between a simple chatbot and RAG?
A simple chatbot follows predefined scripts. A RAG chatbot searches your knowledge base in real-time for accurate answers based on your documents.
